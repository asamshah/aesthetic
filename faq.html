FAQ


How long does each treatment take?

Approximately 30-45 minutes


Do any of the treatment processes hurt?

No. You will feel a slight pinch like you would expect when taking a blood test with a nurse.


What do I need to do before undergoing any of the treatments?

Avoid blood-thinning medication such as Aspirin, Ibuprofen and Fish Oil, one week prior to treatment.

Its important to advise your clinician beforehand, if you have the following -
    * Have an auto-immune disorder
    * Are regularly taking anti-coagulation therapy (i.e.: Warfarin,) Heparin
    * Have an auto-immune disorder
    * Have a severe metabolic or systemic disorder
    * Have active sunburn, eczema or psoriasis
    * Are currently taking aminoglycoside antibiotics
    * Are under 18 years of age (seven-day cooling-off period is required)


Who will NOT be unsuitable or eligible for these treatments?

Anyone that suffers from the following -
    *Myasthenia Gravis or Eaton Lambert Syndrome
    *Is hypersensitive to product ingredients, specifically BTX-A
    *Currently has an active infection in the area to be injected
    *Is pregnant or breastfeeding


What outcome will we see after the treatment?

You should notice a difference after three days with optimum results visible at two weeks.
Results typically last for three to four months.
There will be redness in the treatment area which is usually resolved within 24 hours. Swelling in the treatment area which is usually resolved within three to seven days. You may encounter slight bruising which is usually resolved in seven to ten days.


Do you provide any after-care, post the treatment?

We do. To ensure that you are happy and that optimal results are achieved, it is recommended that you attend a review appointment and assessment after two weeks. Your health and well-being is our priority. If you are concerned about any aspect of your treatment, or if you become unwell – contact us immediately.


What payment plans do you offer?

A deposit must be paid before attending the appointment. This will be discussed during your free consultation. Once the procedure has been completed, we will take the full payment.
